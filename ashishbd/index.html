A Letter Across the Miles 💌

🌷

My Dearest Soul Sister,

There's something almost magical about the fact that we found each other — two people living under completely different skies, in completely different worlds, who somehow just understood each other from the very beginning. 🌸

We've never sat in the same room. We've never had coffee together. We've never walked side by side. And yet — you know me in ways that people who've been around me for years simply don't. That's not distance. That's destiny. 🌍

You are my sister from another mother — not by blood, but by something stronger. By choice. By those 2 AM conversations that went on until dawn. By the voice notes that felt like hugs. By all the little things that quietly built something unbreakable. 🧸

On your birthday, I wish I could be right there with you — to pull you into the longest hug and tell you face to face just how extraordinary you are. But since the miles won't allow it today, please know this: you are so deeply loved, even from here. 💕

One day — I promise — we'll finally meet. And when that day comes, it's going to feel like coming home. Until then, happy birthday my beautiful, brilliant, irreplaceable friend. May today shower you in everything you deserve. 🌺

Yours, from across the world,
Your sibling by soul 🎀
